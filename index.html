Welcome to LacrosseHub!

This is a page that will improve your skill at lacrosse and will provide some fun things to do when you're at home!

Monday And Wednesday shcedule:

  • Warmup: Do a Dynamic Warmup within 10 yards back and forth
  • WallBall: Throw some balls on the wall back and forth and maybe do some tricks while you are at it.
  • Run: Just run as fast as you can back and forth on a feild. This will improve your stamina.
  • CoolDown/Stretch: stretch our your muscles. They need a break.

Tuesday/Thursday schedule:

  • Warmup: Do a Dynamic Warmup within 10 yards back and forth.
  • Skills Work: Partner up if you want and 1v1 to get some dodging and shooting in you.
  • Run: Just Run as fast as you ban back and forth on a feild.
  • CoolDown/Stretch: Stretch your muscles. they need a break.
